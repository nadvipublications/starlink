# SEO Setup Guide - Star Links Advertising LLC

## 1. Verify the domain in Google Search Console

Open Google Search Console, add the domain property for `starlinks.ae`, and verify it using DNS TXT verification through the domain provider. A URL-prefix property for `https://www.starlinks.ae/` can also be added after the domain property.

## 2. Submit the XML sitemap

After verification, submit:

```
https://www.starlinks.ae/sitemap.xml
```

## 3. Request indexing for important pages

Use URL Inspection in Google Search Console for the home page, services page, contact page, and the dedicated service pages. Request indexing after deployment.

## 4. Connect Google Analytics 4

Create a GA4 property, get the Measurement ID, and add the official Google tag to the website head. Do not invent the ID. Replace the placeholder only after the real Measurement ID is available.

## 5. Optimize Google Business Profile

Use the exact business information everywhere:

- Star Links Advertising LLC
- Al Qusais Plaza Building, 6th Floor, Office 613, Al Qusais Industrial Area 2, Deira, Dubai, UAE
- +971 50 6753215
- +971 52 7266343
- https://www.starlinks.ae/

Add real business categories, service descriptions, opening hours, photos, products and service areas.

## 6. Maintain consistent business information

Keep name, address, phone numbers, email and website consistent across the website, Google Business Profile, directories, invoices, social profiles and quotation documents.

## 7. Collect genuine customer reviews

Ask real customers to review completed printing, signage, branding, gift-item or social media projects. Do not buy reviews or publish fake testimonials.

## 8. Track performance

Track keyword impressions, clicks, phone calls, WhatsApp enquiries, quotation-form submissions and page engagement. Compare which service pages create real enquiries.

## 9. Actions requiring external access

These cannot be completed by website code alone: Search Console ownership verification, sitemap submission, indexing requests, GA4 account setup, Google Business Profile verification, review collection and directory citation updates.
